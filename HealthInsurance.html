About Health Insurance

Health insurance is a protection that one buys for themselves and their family to cover their medical and surgical expenses in case need arises. It is an agreement whereby insurance company agrees to undertake a guarantee of compensation for medical expenses in case required.

Rising medical costs and spurt of lifestyle diseases amongst Indians has made it necessary for Indians to buy Health Insurance. A medical emergency can attack anyone, anytime and impact a family emotionally as well as financially. To safeguard ourselves, it is in best interest to buy Health Insurance.

Health Insurance should be bought as soon as possible. The earlier we buy Health Insurance, the more protected our family is and the cheaper the costs turn out to be. Buying Health Insurance at the age of 25 is more than 75% cheaper than buying it at the age of 45. Buy it early to save on your premiums.
